# Contributing to DevDeck

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## License Terms for Contributions

By submitting a contribution (code, docs, assets, or any other material), you agree that:

1. You have the right to submit the contribution.
2. Your contribution will be distributed under the **DevDeck Non-Commercial License** in this repository.
3. The project maintainer may modify and redistribute your contribution under the same license terms.

If you do not agree with these terms, please do not submit a pull request or patch.

## Pull Request Checklist

1. Keep changes focused and minimal.
2. Include clear commit messages and descriptions.
3. Avoid adding unrelated formatting-only changes.
